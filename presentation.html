How it works — 9 steps

  • 1 Search for a restaurant on Google Maps
  • 2 Click "Reserve" → redirected to Reserve on Sui
  • 3 Pick a time slot, select zone, enter contact info
  • 4 Sign in with Google — no wallet app needed
  • 5 Sui address derived from Google identity via zkLogin
  • 6 Gas is sponsored — pay zero SUI
  • 7 Pay the deposit in USDC / USDT / SuiUSD
  • 8 Receive a BookingReceipt NFT on-chain
  • 9 Earn loyalty points, redeem rewards

Built on Sui

Sui Feature How We Use It
zkLogin Google → Sui address. No wallet app, no seed phrase, no crypto knowledge.
Shinami Gas Station Sponsors all gas for zkLogin users. Users pay zero SUI — stablecoin only.
Move Smart Contracts BookingReceipt NFT · PointsLedger · TierRegistry — all on testnet.
PTB Payment + NFT mint in one atomic transaction. Both succeed or neither does.
Stablecoins USDC / USDT / SuiUSD — price-stable deposits. No volatility risk.
Gas-free transfers (mainnet) Sui's native feature — removes sponsorship dependency entirely on mainnet.
